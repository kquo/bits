# Famous Precepts

## Chesterton’s Fence

Do not remove a rule, practice, or institution until you understand why it exists ([Chesterton’s Fence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chesterton%27s_fence)).

No quites una norma, práctica o institución sin antes entender su propósito ([Cerca de Chesterton](https://es.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cerca_de_Chesterton)).
