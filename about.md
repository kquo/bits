# About

`Tips` is a personal reference site.

It exists primarily as a place to keep short notes, reminders, and working views organized for easy reuse. Some entries are practical, some are reflective, and some are argumentative. All of them are snapshots of current thinking rather than final declarations.

## What To Expect

- concise notes over exhaustive treatment
- clear opinions when opinions are being stated
- links to general-reference sources, often Wikipedia, when they help orient the reader
- revisions over time when a view is sharpened, corrected, or replaced

## Organization

The site is organized into four areas:

- [Life](life/index.md)
- [Mind](mind/index.md)
- [Society](society/index.md)
- [Tech](tech/index.md)

## Working Assumption

Accuracy matters, but certainty is limited. The goal is to be honest, reasoned, and open to criticism and revision when better arguments or evidence appear.
